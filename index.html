The Venue

Marzon Hotel Kalibo

Jaime Cardinal Sin Avenue, Andagao, Kalibo, Aklan 5600

A premier destination in the heart of Aklan, offering sophisticated spaces for our grand reunion. Located just minutes from Kalibo International Airport.

Nearby Establishments:

  • Gaisano Capital Kalibo - Shopping & Essentials
  • Kalibo Pastrana Park - Historical Center
  • Bakhawan Eco-Park - Multi-awarded Mangrove Forest

Travel & Registration

Getting There:

From Abroad: Fly to Manila (MNL) or Cebu (CEB), then take a 1-hour domestic flight to Kalibo (KLO).

Local: Direct flights available from Manila and Cebu via Philippine Airlines, Cebu Pacific, or AirAsia.
