Travel & Venue

Marzon Hotel Kalibo

Jaime Cardinal Sin Avenue, Andagao, Kalibo, Aklan 5600

Our primary hub in the heart of Aklan. Located just 10 minutes from Kalibo International Airport. Nearby landmarks: Bakhawan Eco-Park and Gaisano Capital.

How to get there:

  • From Abroad: Fly to Manila (MNL) or Cebu (CEB), then a 1-hour flight to Kalibo (KLO).
  • Regional: Direct flights available via PAL, Cebu Pacific, and AirAsia.
