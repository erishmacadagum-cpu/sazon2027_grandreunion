Guest Concierge

Kalibo Travel Guide

Venue: Marzon Hotel

Address

Jaime Cardinal Sin Avenue, Andagao, Kalibo, Aklan 5600

Room Rates (Est.)

Standard: ₱2,300 - ₱2,800/night
Executive: ₱3,500 - ₱6,000/night

Getting There

Air Travel

Fly to Kalibo International Airport (KLO). The hotel is 5-8 mins from the airport by trike or van.

International Tips

Register on eTravel.gov.ph 72 hours before landing. Passport must be valid for 6 months.

Eat & Explore

Local Eats

Gabriels Food, Late Coffee Cafe, and Gaisano Mall nearby for essentials.

Post-Reunion

Boracay Island is 1.5 hours away via van to Caticlan Jetty Port.
