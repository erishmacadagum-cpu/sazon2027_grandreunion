Marzon Hotel Kalibo: Your Reunion Home

📍 Airport Proximity

Located on Jaime Cardinal Sin Avenue, the hotel is a quick 5-10 minute drive from Kalibo International Airport. Ideal for our relatives flying in from Manila and overseas.

🏊 Resort Facilities

Features a spacious outdoor pool and a renowned in-house restaurant (Latitud). It is widely considered the best venue for large-scale family gatherings in Aklan.

👣 Exploring Aklan

For those arriving early, the hotel is near the Bakhawan Eco-Park—a beautiful 170-hectare mangrove forest with a famous bamboo walkway.
