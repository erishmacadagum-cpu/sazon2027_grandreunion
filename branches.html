Traveler's Guide: Marzon Hotel Kalibo

📍 Location Advantage

Located in Jaime Cardinal Sin Ave. It's just 10 minutes from Kalibo International Airport (KLO). Perfect for family flying in from Manila or abroad.

🏊 Amenities to Enjoy

The hotel features one of the largest swimming pools in Kalibo. Pack your swimwear for the Sazon Family Pool Party!

💡 Local Pro-Tip

The hotel is near the Bakhawan Eco-Park. If you have extra time, the 1.3km bamboo bridge walk through the mangroves is a must-see for the clan.
